What

Hack Upstate is a weekend hackathon where developers, innovators and designers from Upstate New York will come together in Syracuse, NY, share ideas, form teams and build awesome products!

Why

We love to build awesome products at a rapid pace! The Hack Upstate hackathon will provide an opportunity for developers and designers to facilitate innovation, promote creativity and solve challenging problems.
